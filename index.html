Privacy Policy

Kanweos app. Last updated: June 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application "Kanweos" (the "App") handles user information. Developer: Azur Games ("we").

What data we collect

None. The App does not collect, request, store on servers, or share with third parties any personal data. The App has no registration, accounts, or sign-in.

How the values you enter are processed

The amounts, rates, term, and income you enter for calculations are processed locally on your device and are used only to display the result. These values are not sent anywhere and are not stored beyond the current session of the App.

Device permissions

The App does not request access to the camera, microphone, location, contacts, files, call logs, or other sensitive permissions. The App does not require an internet connection to work.

Third-party services, analytics, and advertising

The App contains no advertising, uses no analytics systems, and embeds no third-party tracking SDKs. There are no external services that would receive your data.

Children

The App is not designed to collect data and is suitable for users of any age, since no personal information is collected.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy. A new version will be published on this same page with the date of the update.

Contact

For privacy questions, write to: support@kanweos.me.
